Fiddle Leaf


Particular About Their Care


Real fiddle leaf figs struggle when soil is too wet, do not like soil that is too dry, can dry out from too much air conditioning, and does not like too much sun or not enough sun. So finicky! But so Elegant Looking!

Aloe


The Healer Plant


Ideally placed in full sun, aloe seems to adapt to part shade. The health of the plant may be assessed by looking at its leaves, which, when healthy, should be a vibrant green, plump, feel firm to the touch, and growing upward.
Aloe gel has numerous health benefits including: would healing, sunburn treatment, skincare, and more!

Snake Plant


The Air Purifier


The Snake Plant emits oxygen at night, ideal for bedrooms. Oxygen-rich air improves sleep quality and reduces sleep apnea, helping you sleep soundly and wake refreshed. Not only does the Snake Plant emit oxygen at night, but it also thrives in low light conditions, making it perfect for bedrooms.
